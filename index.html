About The Album

‘Evolve’is a symbol of reinvention. It is the band’s third full-length record in five years, is an ’80s-flecked, power-rock pumping station fit to burst with huge choruses.The single 'Believer' was the first to be released out of the 11 tracks on their latest album. The song’s first verse goes on to color the rest of the album: it speaks of an airing of grievances, then a change of pace.
